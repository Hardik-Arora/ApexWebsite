Solutions

With our Customer centric services, we have served many customers and clients in almost every field and have successfully worked according to their needs and requirements giving priority to the three factors:
1. Product Quality
2. Cost
3. Maintenance

Education

In Educational Institution, we provide services related to Attendance Management Systems, CCTV installations for surveillance of the premises, sound systems, Alarm systems and Biometrics for securing entry and exit points of the Institution.

Hotels and Resorts

Hotels and Resorts have incorporated with highly advanced Gas Leakage Detection Systems, Fire and Intrusion Alarm Systems, CCTV for day and night constant surveillance of the property.

Corporates

We have installed High-Tech products for day and night CCTV surveillance, Fire Alarm Systems, Public Address (PA) systems, EPABX systems, Attendance Management and Biometric Authentiation Systems.

Plants

Fire Alarm, Intrusion Alarm System, CCTV surveillance and EPABX systems have been installed in plants in India and abroad. Plants can also be integrated with Doorlock and Biometric systems.

Government / PSU

Architect and Builder Projects

Architects and Builder projects have been supported by the wide variety of Surveillance products and excellent integration of those products have been done by our skilled professionals.

Individuals

Live sites of individuals, be it Home, Villas or Farmhouse are under surveillance 24*7 to avoid any mishappening through our constant support and integration of high quality products.
